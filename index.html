杨海涛课题组 | IFE Machine Intelligence Lab

杨海涛课题组隶属西北工业大学柔性电子研究院，聚焦软体机器人、柔性电子、传感、通信与计算等方向。

We are an interdisciplinary team that seeks to develop multifunctional flexible/soft electronics to address important scientific and technical challenges in soft machines. Our current research focuses on advanced soft electronic devices for efficient sensing, communicating, and computing that adapt to highly elastic bodies of flexible/soft robots, pushing the boundary of their multifunctionality for dynamic, uncertain tasks.

Affiliations

We are affiliated with the Institute of Flexible Electronics (IFE) , Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU), China.
